Ogisan

A man who let Hodaka to work and give him a place to live in Tokyo. He escape from home when he was in the age of Hodaka, and met his wife. His wife died because of giving birth to their baby. After his wife died he feels sad and losing the energy for living.He felt that Hodaka and Hina was similar with him and his wife.So he give a lot of help to Hodaka, but when the police came to ask him did he know where's Hodaka.He was afraid so he give Hodaka fifty thousand and tell him to leave. At last moment he decided to help Hodaka again so he fight the police.
